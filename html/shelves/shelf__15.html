Once I finally got to a third shelf of books waiting to be read, I knew my personal library was growing into something special. It wasn't just about owning every book I ever read, my personal library was turning into a place where I could curate a collection of books that sounded cool to me! And from there it was obvious that if I was curating a collection, I need to share it! Thus, MNWLIB was born!
