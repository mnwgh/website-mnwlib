As I've mentioned before, my journey of owning every book I ever read started with finding books I loved at the thrift store. I would often find other books that looked interesting when browsing the thrift store book section. And that is mostly what this shelf is. Scattered among these books are some books from Half Price Books or cute little bookstores I may have happened across. Although thrifting books can sometimes be risky, it's always so fun when I get to expand my collection. While I do enjoy a brand new book from time to time, a book that has been read many times by others is just as fun, too!
