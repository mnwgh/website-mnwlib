Dystopian novels were very much IN circa early to mid 2010s. And those were pretty much all I read in high school. I was so invested in stories like The Hunger Games and Divergent that my friends and I would attend the midnight premieres for the movies and wear our favorite merch and just have the best time ever. I still have a small Mockingjay pin from attending midnight premieres. I am actually impressed with the amount of dystopian novels I have read and own. So much so that I consider it it's own genre.
