Just like its sister shelf, this shelf has a theme of book club but now I focus on the men YA authors. This shelf is where the Gordon Korman fan in me shines! The author of my favorite YA heist novel, Swindle, Gordon Korman never fails to capture me with his coming-of-age novels. My middle school book club read quite a number of his books. However, he has a lot of stiff competition on this shelf! With authors like Carl Hiaasen and Louis Sachar, you will not be disappointed with any book on this shelf.
