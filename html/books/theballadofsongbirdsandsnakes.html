by Suzanne Collins

It is the morning of the reaping that will kick of the tenth annual Hunger Games. In the Capitol, eighteen-year-old Coriolanus Snow is preparing for his one shot at glory as a mentor in the Games. The once-mighty house of Snow has fallen on hard times, its fate hanging on the slender chance that Coriolanus will be able to outcharm, outwit and outmaneuver his fellow students to mentor the winning tribute.

The odds are against him. He's been given the humilitating assignment of mentoring the female tribute from District 12, the lowest of the low.
